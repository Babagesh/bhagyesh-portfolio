Research Proposal Project

Assignment Overview

This technical communication assignment required me to develop a comprehensive research proposal addressing a significant problem or question within my groups field of interest. The proposal needed to follow proper academic formatting conventions while clearly conveying the research question, methodology, significance, and expected outcomes in a manner that would be compelling to potential funding sources.

My Approach

When tackling this research proposal, our group first identified a topic that aligns with our interest in artificial intelligence. Before writing, we conducted preliminary research to understand the status of the problem and identify the research gap our proposal would address.

We then structured our proposal to standard academic conventions(APA). We first developed a detailed introduction that established the context and significance of the problem, followed by literature review to demonstrate the understanding of existing work. The methodology section outlined our planned research design, while the budget sections demonstrated the practicality approach.

Revisions Made

After receiving feedback on my groups initial draft, our group implemented several significant revisions to strengthen our proposal:

  • Refined our research question to be more specific, moving from a broad problem to a defined research objective
  • Expanded the methodology section to include more detailed information about our proposed data collection and analysis techniques
  • Addressed language clarity by defining technical terms more effectively and ensuring consistent terminology

Reflections on Technical Communication

Developing this research proposal provided valuable insights into the requirements of academic technical communication. I learned that effective academic writing demands a balance between demonstrating expertise of the subject while avoiding unnecessary complex wording that might confuse readers outside my immediate specialty.

The process taught me the importance of audience analysis in technical communication. Recognizing diverse needs shaped our writing strategy, encouraging our group to develop layered explanations that could satisfy different types of readers.
